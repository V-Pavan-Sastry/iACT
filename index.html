What is PANACEA?


A workshop that develops BRULE (an acronym for Break the RULE) thinking and creative aptitude towards providing solutions for a plethora of industrial problems. The participants will develop skills for understanding problems and contextual learning. This workshop will enable the students to change their mindset. The sessions will take you through the following steps in arriving at distinct solutions

  • How to understand the problem?
  • Gather information
  • Analyze and Interpret
  • Form an explanatory hypothesis.
